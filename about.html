Who we are

Our organisation is working for protecting nature.Our primary focus is to conserve wildlife, ecology, natural resources and sustainable livelihoods for an improved environment.Protect Nature, founded in 2017, is a conservation organisation based in delhi, India.We work for a better future where scientific research, knowledge, community engagement and policy advocacy are put together to achieve sustainable development.

In this fast changing world, conservation is a continuous process where new challenges emerge every day. There is no such milestone in conservation which can be achieved once and for all. However, the process must adjust to social, economic, environmental and scientific advancements. What we do

Awareness:

Protect nature provides awareness about problems and propose sustainable solutions. Research at our organisation often consists of in-depth learning about an environmental problem and then finding suitable solutions.

Education and Training:

We organize programs in schools, companies, etc. to spread knowledge about nature and ways to protect environment. We also provides training in our institute to make people educated about how can we save environment, wildlife, marine eco-system etc.

Pollution Monitoring:

Our Pollution Monitoring Laboratory is an independent, analytical laboratory that monitors toxic contamination of the environment and uses the results of this monitoring to advocate for improved regulation of the use of toxins in the country.

Our Mission

  • To create total awareness about sustainable living and educate people about different ways of conserving the environment by developing behavioural changes.
  • To provide a forum for concerned citizens, NGOs and activists working for the survival of living beings, sustainable development and social change.
  • To provide training to and sharpen the skills of young lawyers, scientists and activists in order to strengthen environmental law and policy.
